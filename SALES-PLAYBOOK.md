## 1. AI vs. Traditional POS: The Value Gap

Standard POS systems in Kenya (SimbaPOS, RobiPOS, etc.) focus on **recording what happened**. Our AI system focuses on **predicting what will happen** — that's where the KES 120,000 value lies.

| Feature | Standard Kenyan POS | Our AI-Powered System | Economic Impact (ROI) |
|---------|--------------------|-----------------------|----------------------|
| **Stock Tracking** | Manual checks & low-stock alerts | Predictive Replenishment: Forecasts demand before it peaks | Reduces "Dead Stock" by up to 20% |
| **Sales Data** | Basic daily/monthly reports | Trend Analysis: Identifies seasonal/external sales drivers | Increases revenue by 15–25% |
| **Waste Control** | Relies on manual expiry tracking | Dynamic Waste Mitigation: Flags slow-movers for early promo | Prevents loss in perishables/fashion |
| **Compliance** | Standard eTIMS/KRA reporting | Integrated Financial Accuracy: Auto-reconciliation of M-Pesa | Eliminates human error & KRA fines |

## 2. The "First Client" Pitch Deck (5 Slides)

Keep it brief and visual. Kenyan SME owners are busy — they want to see the "shillings and cents" impact immediately.

### Slide 1: The Invisible Thief
- **Headline:** Is your inventory "eating" your profits?
- **Content:** Visual of common SME pains — stockouts (lost sales) vs. overstocking (tied-up cash)

### Slide 2: The Solution (Our AI)
- **Headline:** Stop Guessing, Start Predicting.
- **Content:** Briefly explain how our AI integrates with their current POS data to "see" the future

### Slide 3: The 10x ROI Case
- **Headline:** How KES 120k Saves You KES 1.2M.
- **Content:** Use a real example: *"By reducing your waste by just 5% and preventing two stockouts a month, this system pays for itself in 60 days."*

### Slide 4: Implementation Roadmap
- **Headline:** Zero-Downtime Integration.
- **Content:**
  1. Connect to current POS data
  2. 7-day AI learning phase
  3. First automated reorder report

### Slide 5: The "Founder's Offer" (CTA)
- **Headline:** Join our Pilot Program.
- **Content:** First 3 clients get:
  - KES 8,500/month (KES 102k/year)
  - Free 24/7 Priority Support
  - Custom Feature Requests
